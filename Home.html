Attractions In & Around Us

West Hills Mall

West Hills Mall, the largest in West Africa,just moments away from our properties. Its prime location boasts easy access via well-connected roads, making shopping and leisure activities convenient for locals. This proximity enhances community life, fostering economic growth and providing a vibrant hub for residents to shop, dine, and socialize

Bojo Beach Resort

Nestled just a short drive away, the laidback Bojo Beach Resort offers a convenient coastal escape close to our housing community. This gorgeous stretch of golden sand fringed by swaying palm trees hosts an array of lively beach resorts and bars. Spend your days indulging in water sports like jet-skiing and banana boat rides, or simply soak up the sun while sipping refreshing coconut cocktails. As the sun sets, the night's scene comes alive with beachside bonfires, drumming circles, and a vibrant energy that keeps everyone dancing into the wee hours.

Babibanny Go-Karting

Get ready for an adrenaline-fueled adventure at Babibanny's Go-Karting, thrilling go-kart course conveniently located just around the corner! This dynamic racing circuit offers the perfect family-friendly experience to get everyone's heart racing. Kids will squeal with delight as they zip around the twists and turns behind the wheel of their very own speedy go-kart. With multiple skill levels to choose from, even young racers can join in on the fun. Cheer each other on from the sidelines and embrace the competitive spirit as you battle for the fastest lap times. After catching your breath, refuel at the snack bar before heading back onto the track. For an exhilarating day of high-octane excitement awaits!
